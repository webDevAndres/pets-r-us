Dog Training

Pets-R-Us dog training lessons can teach your dog how to act properly at home and while traveling, regardless of whether you have a young puppy, an older dog, or a rescue. Positive reinforcement is used by our accredited pet trainers to deliver engaging and successful group and private lessons.

Puppy Level 1

This 6-week introductory group class focuses on proper socialization and teaches desired behaviors and cues to address common puppy challenges, such as:

  • Potty Training
  • Chewing and Teething
  • Digging and More

For puppies 2-4 months

$100

Puppy Level 2

This 6-week intermediate group class expands on the behaviors and cues learned in Puppy Level 1, introducing:

  • Learning how to establish behaviors as routines
  • Introduction to fun games to mentally stimulate your puppy
  • Advanced training cues

For puppies 4-6 months

$150

Potty Training Seminar

This results-focused one-hour seminar covers effective potty-training tips and techniques for puppies or dogs of any age.

$75

Young Adult Level 1

This 6-week introductory group class is designed to introduce techniques to reinforce behaviors learned in our level 1 course while continuing to build on establishing a daily routine.

For dogs that have completed our puppy level courses

$100

Young Adult Level 2

This 6-week intermediate group class expands on behaviors and cues learned in Young Adult Level 1, introducing:

  • Introduction to alone play
  • Adding more distance, duration and distractions to help reinforce behaviors

For dogs that completed our Young Adult level 1 course

$150

Adult Level 1

This 6-week introductory group class is designed to help you better communicate with and train your dog. Dogs will learn behaviors & cues, such as:

  • Drop it
  • Wait
  • Loose Leash walking
  • tips to help control jumping, barking and digging

For dogs over 6 months

$100

Adult Level 2

This 6-week intermediate group class expands on behaviors and cues learned in Adult Level 1, introducing:

  • Introduction to advanced training techniques
  • Advanced fun games to mentally stimulate your dog

For dogs that completed our Adult level 1 course

$150

Separation Anxiety Course

This four-week group program is designed to teach your dog how to be at home alone and relax while addressing separation anxiety. Your dog will pick up on:

  • How to relax when you are away
  • Techniques to calm themselves when stressed
  • Developing healthy routines

$75

Meet our Trainers

Sara - Trainer

Sara knew dogs would always be a part of his life after working at a veterinary clinic and doing volunteer work for rescue organizations. He takes pleasure in bringing out the lively puppy at the core of a scared dog. He specializes in forming deep bonds with pets.

Mike - Trainer

Since his father brought home a yellow Labrador when he was three, Mike has had an obsession with working dogs. he was in charge of three dogs, two birds, and a horse by the time he started high school. Mike trained in a number of dog sports after graduation, including agility, flyball, treibball, barn hunt, nose work, tricks, rally obedience, and weight pull, as he was keen to further his understanding of the canine world.
