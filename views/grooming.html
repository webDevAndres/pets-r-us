Our Process

Depending on your dog's size, coat type, and the services you select, our grooming appointments might last anywhere between two and four hours.


1. Five to ten minutes for a hands-on pet assessment

Your pet's skin, coat, ears, nails, and teeth will be evaluated by one of our safety-certified salon staff so that their specific needs may be met. While our stylists do keep an eye out for any behavioral or health issues, this is a perfect opportunity to let us know of any other concerns, preferences, or unique requirements.


2. Brushing and trimming (10â€“30 minutes) (pre-bath steps

Before giving your pet a bath, a pet stylist will first brush, comb, and clip any extra hair or tangles off them. They will also clean the pet's ears and cut its nails.


3. A soothing bath: 10 to 30 minutes

Bath time becomes a peaceful experience thanks to Pets-R-Us's soothing bath system. Your pet will first be washed by your salon professional using the shampoo and conditioner that you chose at check-in. Our entire line of shampoos and conditioners is pH-balanced specifically for pets and offers gentle washing without irritating your pet's skin.


4. Special Drying: More than 30 minutes

Your pet's face, ears, and tail will first be gently dried with a cloth. Our dryers have varying speeds and are not heated. Each pet is carefully dried using the best way for safety and comfort because various breeds have varied needs.


5. Customized Haircut & Finishing Touches: 30 minutes or longer

After your pet has been washed and blow-dried, your stylist may guarantee there are no further tangles and give them the best-looking haircut by finishing the haircut. If your pet merely had a wash or de-shedding service, this time will be used to finish brushing them, touch up their nails, clean their ears, and make any final preparations before they are picked up.
